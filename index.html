About

Hi, I am a M.S. student in User & Information Lab (U&I Lab), KAIST. I am currently interested in NLP application, especially in English education. I believe generative AI holds the potential to innovate the field of education, offering enhanced learning and teaching experience for both students and instructors. With a focus on educational domain, I am eager to explore the capabilities that AI can bring to this field.
